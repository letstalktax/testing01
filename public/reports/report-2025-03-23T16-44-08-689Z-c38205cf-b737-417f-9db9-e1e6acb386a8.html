5. UAE Corporate Tax Analysis

5.1. Taxable Person Status

Gourmet Gulf Co LLC is registered in Dubai, UAE as a Limited Liability Company. Based on the UAE Corporate Tax Law, specifically Article 11(3)(a), a company incorporated or otherwise established or recognised under the applicable legislation in the UAE is considered a Resident Person. As Gourmet Gulf Co LLC is a Limited Liability Company registered in Dubai, it meets the definition of a Resident Juridical Person. Therefore, Gourmet Gulf Co LLC is a Resident Juridical Person for UAE Corporate Tax purposes.

5.3.5. Loss Carry Forward

Analysis: Gourmet Gulf Co LLC has accumulated losses of AED 55,763,079 as of 31 December 2023. Article 37 of the UAE Corporate Tax Law allows for the carry forward of Tax Losses to offset future Taxable Income, subject to conditions.

Potential Corporate Tax Adjustments & Considerations:

  • Loss Utilization Limit: Tax Losses can be carried forward and used to offset up to 75% of Taxable Income in future Tax Periods.
  • Loss Expiry: Carried forward Tax Losses do not expire and can be used indefinitely, provided conditions are met.
  • Conditions for Carry Forward: Continuity of ownership or Business continuity tests under Article 39 must be met to utilize Tax Losses carried forward. Assess if changes in ownership or business activity impact loss carry forward eligibility.

Action Required:

  • Loss Tracking: Maintain detailed records of accumulated Tax Losses and ensure proper tracking of carry forward amounts.
  • Eligibility Assessment: Annually assess the company's eligibility to carry forward and utilize Tax Losses, considering ownership and business continuity requirements.
  • Documentation: Document all Tax Loss calculations, carry forward schedules, and assessments of eligibility.
